# Stage 01: Scoping

Define the research goal, scope, and methodology.

## Inputs

| Source | File/Location | Why |
|--------|---------------|-----|
| User | (conversation) | Research question OR topic OR source materials |
| Brand vault | `../../brand-vault.md` | Preferences |
| Reference | `references/methodology-guide.md` | Available methodologies |

## Process

1. Determine input type: research question, topic, or source materials
2. If topic: expand into 3-5 specific research questions
3. If sources: analyze for gaps and generate questions
4. Define research scope: boundaries, exclusions, depth vs breadth
5. Select methodology from guide (literature review, case study, comparative, etc.)
6. Identify 3-7 key questions the research must answer
7. Estimate timeline and resource needs
8. **[Checkpoint]** Present scope document to user for approval
9. Run audit checks, revise if needed
10. Write research goal document to output/

## Checkpoints

| After Step | Agent Presents | Human Decides |
|------------|---------------|---------------|
| 7 | Research scope with goal, questions, methodology, boundaries | Whether scope and methodology are appropriate |

## Verifiability

**Classification:** EXPERT-VERIFIABLE

**Verification Method:** Methodology match to topic, question quality assessment

**Human Review Trigger:** Always required (scope approval checkpoint)

## Audit

| Check | Pass Condition |
|-------|---------------|
| Clear research goal | Goal statement is specific and answerable |
| Key questions defined | At least 3 research questions identified |
| Methodology selected | One methodology chosen and justified |
| Scope boundaries | In/out of scope explicitly stated |

## Outputs

| Artifact | Location | Format |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Research goal | `output/research-goal.md` | Goal, key questions, methodology, scope, timeline |
