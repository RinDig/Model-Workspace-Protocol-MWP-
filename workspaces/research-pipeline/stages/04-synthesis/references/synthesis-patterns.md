# Synthesis Patterns

Combine findings into conclusions.

## Pattern Detection
- **Convergence:** Multiple sources agree → Strong conclusion
- **Divergence:** Sources disagree → Present both views
- **Gap:** No sources address → Flag for future research
- **Novelty:** Unique finding → Verify with additional sources

## Handling Contradictions
- **Reconcile:** Find variable that explains difference
- **Contextualize:** Explain when each finding applies
- **Prioritize:** Weight by evidence strength
- **Acknowledge:** State that contradiction exists without resolution

## Confidence Levels
- **High:** Multiple strong sources agree
- **Medium:** Some evidence supports, gaps exist
- **Low:** Limited evidence, further research needed
