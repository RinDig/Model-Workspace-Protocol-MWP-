# Synthesis Process

Combine findings into coherent conclusions.

## Process
1. Read analyzed findings from 03-analysis
2. Group findings by research question
3. Rank findings by source credibility
4. Detect contradictions (document conflicts)
5. Resolve contradictions (credibility-weighted)
6. Identify gaps (unanswered questions)
7. Structure coherent narrative
8. Assign confidence levels to each conclusion

9. Write synthesized knowledge document

## Confidence Levels
- **High:** Multiple strong sources agree
- **Medium:** Some evidence supports, gaps exist
- **Low:** Limited evidence, further research needed
